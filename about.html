Our Mission

Years ago, our founders experienced the pain of building internet businesses from outside the US. Virtual mailboxes led to closed bank accounts. VoIP numbers led to Stripe suspensions. "Compliance" felt like a tax purely for being international.

We realized that the fundamental missing piece wasn't a better software dashboard—it was real, tangible, legally-sound infrastructure.

We built The Residential Address to solve this exact problem. By securitizing physical residential leases in business-friendly states like Wyoming and Delaware, we enable international entrepreneurs to establish a 100% compliant, bank-approved presence in the United States.

Today, we serve hundreds of founders across 40+ countries—providing the LLCs, ITINs, Addresses, and Banking support necessary to scale their global ambitions without the fear of sudden account closures.
