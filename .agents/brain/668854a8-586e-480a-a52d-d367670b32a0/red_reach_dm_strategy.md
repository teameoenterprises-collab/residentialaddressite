## 📝 Anti-Ban & Safety Protocol

### 1. The "Spintax" Rule
- **Concept**: Never send the exact same message twice.
- **Mechanism**: Use variations: `{Hey|Hi|Hi there} I saw your {thread|post|comment}...`
- **Result**: Messages look unique and "alive" to Reddit's abuse detection systems.

### 2. Volume & Pacing
- **Daily Limits**:
    - **New Accounts**: 25 DMs/day max.
    - **Aged/Karma Accounts**: 50-60 DMs/day.
- **Delays**: Minimum 2-second delay between messages to avoid "bot-like" velocity pings.

### 3. Nurturing vs. Spamming (The "Nudge" Strategy)
- **First Message**: Goal is a **response**, not a click. 
- **Rule**: Do NOT include links in the first message. It's a high signal for spam.
- **The Hook**: "I saw you were struggling with X. I build a tool for that. Do you want to hear more?"
- **The Close**: Only send the link (Calendly/Solution) after they reply.
