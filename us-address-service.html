Why Your Address Gets You Rejected

US banks cross-reference every business address against federal databases. Standard virtual mailboxes are flagged instantly.

CMRA Flagged Addresses

Standard virtual mailbox providers (EarthClassMail, iPostal1) are registered as CMRA (Commercial Mail Receiving Agency) addresses. Mercury, Relay, and Stripe automatically reject these during KYC verification.

Our Fix: We provide addresses that are NOT in the CMRA database.

PO Boxes Don't Pass KYC

Banks require a physical street address for business banking applications. PO Boxes and "Suite" numbers at UPS stores are automatically flagged as non-residential.

Our Fix: Each client gets a unique residential unit address with a proper lease agreement.

Shared Addresses = Shut Down

When hundreds of businesses register at the same address, the address gets blacklisted. Banks use aggregator databases to detect this pattern.

Our Fix: Limited per-property occupancy ensures your address stays clean and passes verification long-term.

Address FAQ

Is this a real physical address or a virtual one?

+

It's a real, physical residential property in Wyoming. Unlike virtual mailbox services that use CMRA-registered commercial addresses, our addresses are genuine residential locations with individual unit designations. This is the critical difference that allows you to pass strict banking KYC checks.

Can I use this address for my LLC registration?

+

Yes. You can use this address as your LLC's principal office address, mailing address, and the address listed on banking applications. However, your Registered Agent address must be a separate Wyoming-registered agent (which we also provide as part of our LLC formation package).

How does mail scanning work?

+

When mail arrives at your address, we scan the envelope exterior and notify you via email or WhatsApp. You can then request us to open and scan the contents in high-resolution, shred it, or forward the physical item to any international address. Most scans are delivered within 2-4 hours of receipt.
