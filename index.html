FAQ

Compliance FAQ

The real questions founders Google before they buy.

Can I use a virtual mailbox to open a US bank account?
No. Most U.S. banks (Capital One, Chase, Mercury, Alliant) scan your address against the USPS CMRA database. Virtual mailboxes are flagged as Commercial Mail Receiving Agencies and are automatically rejected. You need a real residential address with lease documentation.
What documents does Capital One require for non-US residents?
Capital One requires: (1) A valid ITIN, (2) A valid passport, (3) A legitimate, real residential address, (4) A completed W-8BEN form, and (5) A valid passport ID.
Will banks accept my application if I'm based in India?
Yes. If your personal profile has a compliant U.S. address (residential lease), a valid ITIN, and a U.S. bank account. We've helped 30+ Indian founders get their banks verified and active this quarter alone.
What's the difference between a registered agent address and a residential address?
A registered agent address is only for receiving legal/state documents—it cannot be used as your address for banks or Stripe. A residential address is a real, physical address with a signed lease that passes bank verification (KYC/AML checks).
Do I need an ITIN to open a U.S. bank account?
Not always for business accounts. Some banks (like Capital One) accept EIN-only applications for LLCs. However, having an ITIN significantly expands your options and is required for personal banking, credit building, and certain payment processors. We offer ITIN applications for $400.
Is your residential address compliant with U.S. banking regulations?
Yes. Our addresses are real residential units with legally signed leases. They are not flagged in the USPS CMRA database, they pass Patriot Act verification, and they include utility bills in your LLC name. If any bank rejects your address, we replace it free of charge or refund you 100%.
Can I use this address for a Google Business Profile?
Yes! Unlike virtual mailbox services that restrict Google Business Profile usage, our residential addresses fully support GBP listing. You'll receive mail verification postcards at your address, which we forward to you.
What happens if my address is rejected by a bank?
If any U.S. bank or payment processor rejects your residential address, we'll replace it with a new address free of charge or refund you 100%—no questions asked. We have a 100% success rate so far across 30+ founder applications.
Which is better for my LLC: Wyoming or Delaware?
For most international founders, Wyoming is better: $0 state income tax, a $60/year annual report, strong privacy laws, and no franchise tax. Delaware charges $300/year in franchise tax and has more complex compliance. We recommend Wyoming unless you plan to seek U.S. venture capital funding (in which case, Delaware is preferred).
How long does ITIN processing take?
Standard ITIN processing by the IRS takes 7–11 weeks. We prepare and submit your W-7 application with all supporting documentation. The $400 fee covers our preparation, review, and filing service.
